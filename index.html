THE PAST

As a child born in the 80s, young European boy was still not able to get completely hooked on the Internet in his early days. And so the boy shown interest in other activities. Mostly, reverse engineering (or simply dissassambling) every man or woman made product. Was it powered by electricity? Great, more sparkling reasons to take it apart. Naturally, when Internet hit our bills he was hooked.

He moved to Scotland to graduate from Computer Science where he also gained his master degree. Designed, developed and maintained many websites, web applications and AI projects. Scottish weather is harsh. One rainy morning he said enough and moved to Amsterdam. If you think Dutch weather is not much better, believe me, it is wee better than you may think.

He joined tech startup in Amsterdam then co-founded another one.
