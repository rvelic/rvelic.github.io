THE PAST

As a child born in the 80s, the young boy was still not able to get completely hooked on the Internet in his early days. And so, the boy showed interest in other activities. Mostly, reverse engineering (or simply disassembling) every man or woman made product. Was it powered by electricity? Great, more sparkling reasons to take it apart. Naturally, when Internet hit our bills he was hooked.

Roman moved to Scotland to graduate from Computer Science where he also gained his master’s degree. Designed, developed and maintained many webs, apps and couple of AI projects. One rainy morning he moved to Amsterdam - the international hub for new ideas, tech & enterprise. He joined tech startups then co-founded another one.
